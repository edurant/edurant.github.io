Flavor Craver Dip

  • 1/4 (?) cup Heinz Chili Sauce
  • 1 package (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 teaspoons horseradish
  • 1 can (4 1/2 oz.) tiny shrimp, rinsed and drained

Blend Heinz Chili Sauce gradually into cream cheese. Mix in mayonnaise, onion and horseradish. Fold in shrimp. Cover and chill at least 1 hour.
