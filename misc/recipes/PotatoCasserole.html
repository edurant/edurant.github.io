Potato Casserole

  • 2# frozen hash browns
  • ½ c. milk
  • ¼ t. pepper
  • ¼ c. minced onion
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 2 c. grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 c. melted butter mixed with 1 c. breadcrumbs

Defrost potatoes. Combine soup and milk. Add all other ingredients except buttered breadcrumbs. Use a 3 qt. casserole sprayed with Pam™. Top with buttered breadcrumbs. Bake at 350º for 1 hour.
