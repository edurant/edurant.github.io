Harvey Wallbanger Cake

  • 1 pkg. yellow cake mix
  • 1 pkg. vanilla instant pudding
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 4 eggs
  • ¼ C. vodka
  • ¼ C. galliano
  • ½ C. orange juice

Mix all together and beat for 4 min. Pour into a well-greased and lightly floured bundt pan. Bake 40-45 minutes. Dust with powdered sugar. (Temp. 350°F)
