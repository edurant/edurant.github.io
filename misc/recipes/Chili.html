Chili

Ingredients

  • 1# chop meat
  • 1 large can tomato sauce
  • 1 can light kidney beans
  • 1 can dark kidney beans
  • 1 C or more cooked noodles
  • 1 white or yellow onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 t or more chili powder

Instructions

  • Put sauce in pot, set to low heat.
  • Add chili powder.
  • Fry and drain chop meat and add to pot.
  • Rinse beans and add to pot.
  • Dice onion and pepper, microwave with about 1/2 C water to tenderize, drain, and add to pot.
  • Cook noodles and add to pot.

Makes 3 large servings
