--- title: "CS-489 Cycle 1 Design Report" ---

The Cycle 1 design document should contain the following items:

  1. Title page, including project name, team number, and names of team members.
  2. Table of contents.
  3. Brief project description.
  4. Real use case(s) for Cycle 1.
  5. Interaction (Sequence or Collaboration) diagrams (CASE Tool).
  6. Design class diagrams (CASE Tool).
  7. Glossary of terms:
    • Similar in concept to that described in the textbook, though you may use any reasonable format.
    • This glossary should be expanded and updated throughout the project.
  8. Issue list:
    • A list of questions to be asked, decisions to be made, topics to be investigated, etc.
    • This list is to be maintained throughout the project; items should be marked "complete", dated, and explained when they are resolved, but not removed from the list.
  9. Contingency plan
    • What might go wrong and how do you plan to address it.
  10. Project management information (see process details):
    • Time spent on the project to date, by team member and phase. Include summary totals by team member, by phase, and for the entire project.
    • Copies of the time tracking sheets (or other form of recording) of all team members.
  11. Your CASE tool model file.
