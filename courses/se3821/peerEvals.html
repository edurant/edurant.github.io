--- title: "SE-3821 Software Requirements and Specification: Project Team Peer Reviews" ---

Upon completion of the project, each team member must complete the Peer Review Form in Excel and turn it in via email by 4 P.M. on Monday of week 11.

Please note that your comments on other team members will be shared with them, although all feedback about a team member from other team members will be combined and individual commentor names will not be supplied.

You should also feel free to include private comments to the professor in the body of your email, but you are encouraged to provide candid feedback to your teammates.
