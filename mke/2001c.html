St. Boniface Episcopal Church (Mequon)
3906 W. Mequon Rd.
2001

This new, nearly $2.6 million church, is clad almost completely in copper sheeting. It was designed by Jim Shields of Hammel, Green & Abrahamson, and replaces the former building, constructed in phases between the 1950s and the 1980s. The interior includes Douglas fir ceilings, cedar pews, Vermont slate floor, and a limestone altar.
