Milwaukee Intermodal Station (Amtrak Passenger Station)
433 W. St. Paul Ave.
1965/2007

This facility was needed after both primary railroad stations were torn down (Milwaukee Road in 1965, and Chicago & Northwestern in 1968). The facades include semicircular arches with dark tinted glass windows surrounded by glazed brown brick. The 125 foot steel tower holds an electronic bell.

It was rebuilt as the Milwaukee Intermodal Station in 2007.
