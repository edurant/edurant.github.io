East Center Street Natatorium
243 E. Center St.
1908

Natatoria were constructed to provide public baths, in an era of increasingly polluted waterways. Many people learned to swim at neighborhood natatoria. Others were located at: 1646 S. 4th St., 1609 W. North Ave., 1645 W. Greenfield Ave. and 2361 S. 10th St.
