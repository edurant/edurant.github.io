Allen-Bradley (Rockwell Automation) Clocktower
1201 S. Second St.
1962

Said to be the largest 4-sided clock in the world, this is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Milwaukee. Some facts about the clocks: Each weighs over 12 tons; each face is 40' 3.5" wide; hour hands are 15' 9" long and weigh 490 pounds; minute hands are 20' long and weigh 530 pounds. Rockwell Automation is an internationally known manufacturer of electronic equipment and components.
