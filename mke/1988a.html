Bradley Center
1001 N. Fourth St.
1988

This is the home of the professional sports teams the Milwaukee Bucks (basketball) and Milwaukee Admirals (hockey). The exterior has over 110,000 square feet of rose-colored cornelian granite and is topped with leaded copper. The venue, which holds 20,000 visitors, honors Milwaukee industrialist Harry Lynde Bradley, co-founder of the Allen-Bradley Co. It was a gift to the community from Bradley's daughter, Jane Pettit, and her husband, Lloyd.
