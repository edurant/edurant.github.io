Eline Chocolate Factory (Barnabas Building Properties)
4650 N. Port Washington Rd. (Glendale)
1922

This substantial and attractive 270' long office building was the administrative offices for the Eline Chocolate Co., a business created by the Uihlein family of Milwaukee. It was designed by Kirchhoff & Rose, Architects.

Over the years it saw use as a munitions plant and several schools. It is now a multi-tenant office operated by Barnabas Building Properties.

Images show the whole building and also the gatehouse to the property.
