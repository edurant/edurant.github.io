Richard D. Whitehead Horse Watering Trough
S. 16th, S. Pearl, W. Bow Sts.
1910

Inscription on back reads: "Erected by R.D. Whitehead, Supt. of the Badger State Humane Society in rememberance of my faithful friends. Horse: George -- Dogs: Dandy, Pet, Punch, Judy & Shorty -- Cats: Frank & Henry -- Birds: Browney & Dick."
