A.F. Gallun (Pritzlaff) Mansion
3000 E. Newberry Blvd.
1914

This Tudor mansion is built of Bedford limestone, with copper gutters and a slate roof. The owner was the president of the Gallun Tannery. After Gallun's death, the home was inherited by his daughter, wife of hardware store executive John Pritzlaff Sr. Suffering from years of decay, it is now restored to its original beauty.
