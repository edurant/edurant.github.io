Hoan Bridge
Milwaukee River at Lake Michigan
1974

This bridge design, by Howard Needles, Tammen & Bergendorff, was one of 11 designs submitted for the project and in 1975 was awarded the Long Span Bridge Award by the American Institute of Steel Construction. The bridge is approximately 120' above the water.

On 12/13/00, it suffered severe damage as two of three main girders developed major cracks (one cracking through completely) and was closed for months.
