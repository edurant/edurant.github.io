Charles Allis Art Museum
1630 E. Royall Place
1909

Alexander Eschweiler designed this red/purple brick and sandstone mansion for Charles Allis, the first president of the Allis Chalmers Manufacturing Co. The elegant interior features beautiful walnut and mahogany panelling. Mrs. Allis willed the property to the City of Milwaukee in 1945, and it is now used as an art museum.
