Office Hours

My office is in CC27. Office hours are listed above and by appointment. Students may make appointments during office hours, so, if you know in advance that you would like to see me at a specific time, you may wish to make an appointment. If you do not have an appointment, please come during the first 25 minutes of an office hour; if nobody comes during the first 25 minutes, I may be elsewhere in the building. Appointments may be available during times designated PD, prep., or grading with sufficient notice. I am also available in my office for walk-in traffic at many times.
